# Part I
File types and conventions are not just code—they are tools. As such, they are designed objects that have life as a social technology as well as their functional technology. 

This understanding is particularly important for FOSS (Free and Open Source Software) and the types of conventions (like EPUBs) that it uses. For a tool to be convivial (and for us to be convivial toolmakers), we need to understand the social system *around* a piece of technology. 

This section gives a high level overview of the historical and social forces that has shaped the EPUB format up to 2026. It seeks to be a shallow history—just enough information to serve as a foundation for further inquiry.
