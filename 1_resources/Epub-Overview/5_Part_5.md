# Part V
Part IV contains some additional information for the world outside the EPUB file. EPUBs are practical files that—like all tools—must live in ecosystems where real humans use them in combinations with other tools to facilitate learning, entertainment, and personal growth. 

The ecosystem around EPUBs includes the devices they run on (5.1), tools for creating and editing epubs (5.2), considerations for internationalization (5.3), and artifacts that are created by readers reading epubs, including highlights, annotations, and more (5.4).
